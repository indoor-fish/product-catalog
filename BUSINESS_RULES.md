# Product Catalog — Business Rules

## Rule 1: Merchant Discount
MERCHANT tier customers receive a **15% discount** on orders where the total exceeds **$500**. This discount is applied at the pricing tier and is calculated on the base product price.

## Rule 2: Clearance Category Lock
Products in the **`clearance`** category cannot have additional discount codes applied. The clearance price is already the final price. This prevents double-discounting on end-of-life stock.

## Rule 3: Scarcity Surcharge
When a product's available inventory falls below **10 units**, an automatic **8% price increase** is applied. This reflects supply scarcity and incentivises early purchase.
